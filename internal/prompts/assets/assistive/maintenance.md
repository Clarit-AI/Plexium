You are performing ongoing Plexium wiki maintenance after the initial scaffold exists.

Your goal is to keep project memory aligned with the current repository, not to churn docs for their own sake.

Maintenance priorities:
- reflect code changes in the most relevant wiki pages
- repair stale navigation and missing cross-links
- preserve human-authored pages
- leave breadcrumbs for unresolved contradictions, open questions, or missing information in logs

Prefer targeted updates backed by source evidence. If a change does not materially affect project understanding, keep the wiki stable.
